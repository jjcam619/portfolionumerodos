About Me

Justin Campbell was born and raised in San Diego, California. It was there where he attended both Torrey Pines High School and continued to graduate from Clairemont Senior High School. He then continued his education attending Miramar Community college and UCSD Extension to obtain the necessary skills needed for software and website development.

For the past 7 years Justin has been working in Healthcare with internationally facilities recognized for excellence such as Sharp Healthcare and UCSD Health. His duties ranged from medical coding, insurance processing and billing, all the way to the ultimate goal of patient care and patient satisfaction.
